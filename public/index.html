Marius Wilms

As an engineering leader and passionate Open Source engineer I enjoy to collaborate with people all around the world.

A central theme of my work is the infrastructure that software engineers work with - in a literal and metaphorical sense.

I've built tools such as frameworks, libraries, toolchains. I support an engineering culture where working on eye level accross disciplines feels natural. I educate and find appropriate processes, to bring teams in resonance with the larger business. I provide guidance and mentorship for personal growth.

The perfect development environment has yet to be found, I'm convinced how we create software today can still be improved.
